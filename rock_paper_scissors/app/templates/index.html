Rules of the Game

Select Play Game to start a new game

Enter your name in the player1 box (typing over player1) and press ENTER

You will be playing against the computer. The computer will randomly select an choice after you have selected your choice.

    ROCK beats SCISSORS

    PAPER beats ROCK

    SCISSORS beats PAPER

    In the event that you and the computer select the same choice, it will be a draw.
